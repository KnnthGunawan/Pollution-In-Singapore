Singapore contributes around 0.1 per cent of global emissions. Most of Singapore's emissions are produced in the industry sector, followed by the power sector. (Click on the pie chart to see how much!)

Singapore's strategic position along the East-West trade routes has made the country a natural location for oil storage and refining facilities.

Using the nation's position as a key regional port, the refining and petrochemical plants help create good business relationships with countries in Southeast Asia, the Western Pacific, South Asia and Australasia.
